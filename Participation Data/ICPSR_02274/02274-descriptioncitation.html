Common Core of Data: Local Education Agencies (LEA) Nonfiscal Data, 1983-1984 (ICPSR 2274)

Principal Investigator(s): United States Department of Education. National Center for Education Statistics

Project Description:

Summary: This data collection contains data on students and staffing of local education agencies (LEA). Included are counts of the number of schools, students, teachers, instructional aides, and other staff for public school districts in each of the 50 states, the District of Columbia, and outlying territories (American Samoa, Guam, Puerto Rico, the Virgin Islands, and the Marshall Islands). Data for teachers, aides, and, where possible, other staff are in full-time equivalents (FTEs).
